Account & Data Deletion — Future Focus Test

Use this page to request permanent deletion of your account and associated personal data stored by the Future Focus Test app.

How to request deletion

To request deletion of your account and associated data, please follow one of the methods below. We require the registered email address (or account ID) to locate and remove your records.

  1. Email — Send an email to futurefocustest@gmail.com with subject line Account Deletion Request. Include your registered email address and (optional) full name.
  2. Postal/Phone — You may also call or visit our support numbers for verification if required: 9507091174 • 8603497015 • 7258859064
Example email body:
Subject: Account Deletion Request
Registered email: youremail@example.com
Name (optional): Your Name
We will confirm receipt of your request by email. For security, we may ask a short verification question before deleting account data.

Data that will be deleted

  • Email address and user profile (name)
  • Test results, scores and learning progress
  • Authentication records stored in Firebase (user UID)
  • Associated user documents in Firestore

Data we may retain temporarily

For security, fraud prevention and backup purposes, certain logs or backups may be retained for a short period before permanent deletion (up to 30 days).

Processing time

We will process deletion requests within 7 business days. You will receive an email confirmation once deletion is complete.
